About Samba

Samba is the Web Design Workshop of Eng Break'17 academic project

In Samba we learn how to become front-end developers with designing background using web design tools such as HTML, CSS and JQuery.

The workshop is moderated by Maha Taha, Khaled Badr, Basant Medhat and Shrouk Sobhy.

Samba's participants are known for their passion and enthusiasm to learn, they're also very creative and helpful.

About Eng Break

Eng Break is a student activity that's founded in Faculty of Engineering Cairo University

Eng Break's Vision is investing student's time to enrich their culture, experience and thoughts and its mission is providing creative projects focusing on values of pioneers generation
